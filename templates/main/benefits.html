Benefits for members

Below are the membership discounts as listed on the ANUMC website. This information is reproduced word for word from the public page at Benefits for members to ensure nothing is summarised. Please consult the original site for the most up‑to‑date details.

Membership Discounts:

The following stores are generous enough to provide ANUMC member discounts. If you’re in the market for gear, please consider buying from them. You’ll need to show your ANUMC membership card in order to get the discount. If you have any trouble, please let someone on the Executive know.

Bloc Haus:

$17 casual entry at all BlocHaus gyms
20% off 3‑month, 6‑month & 12‑month paid in full memberships

https://cbr.blochaus.com.au/

Mountain Strong:

Participants on organised Club trips will also receive a 20% ($15) ‑ 30% ($18) discount on entry and shoe hire!

On private trips:

10% off memberships and 5 or 10 passes
