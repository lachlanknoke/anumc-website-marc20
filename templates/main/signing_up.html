Signing Up

Ready to join the ANU Mountaineering Club? Follow these steps to become a member and start signing up for trips:

  1. Join ANU Sport: Visit the ANU Sport website and create a free account. Select “ANU Mountaineering Club” as one of your clubs and pay the membership fee (see FAQs for pricing). Keep a copy of your UniSport receipt.
  2. Email your receipt: Send your receipt, your full name and the email you used to register to anumountaineeringclub@gmail.com. Our executive volunteers will manually activate your ANUMC account. This process usually takes 1–2 business days.
  3. Activate your ANUMC account: Once your account is created you will receive a confirmation email. Follow the link in the email to set your password and log in to the ANUMC website.
  4. Sign up for trips: After logging in, browse the Trip Calendar and click “Sign up” on trips you’re interested in. Make sure you meet any skill or fitness requirements listed.

Having trouble? Sometimes the automatic account creation fails (e.g. if ANU Sport’s website is down). If you haven’t received a confirmation email within a few days, or if you can’t log in after paying, please email us with your receipt and we will set up your account manually. During busy periods there may be a short delay; thanks for your patience!
